6 questions about Just Work Comp Law.

Is My Injury Work Related?

Yes, if it happened during the course of your employment.

Can I Represent My Own Case?

Yes, but it is advised to have an attorney specializing in Workers Compensation Law.

How to Choose the Best Lawyer

It is important to find a lawyer who specializes in workers compensation, has a strong track record, and prioritizes communication.

Why Was My Claim Denied?

There are many reasons a claim can be denied, but most often it is on the basis of causality (whether the injury actually arose from work), or a factual dispute.

What's an Independent Medical Eval?

An Independent Medical Evaluation (IME) is a medical examination performed by a doctor who has not been previously involved in your care. This evaluation is typically requested by the insurance company to provide an objective assessment of your medical condition and its relation to your work injury.

My Injury Got Worse, Now What?

If your work injury has worsened, you may be entitled to additional benefits. It is crucial to notify your employer and seek medical attention as soon as possible. Consulting with a workers' compensation attorney can help you understand your rights and navigate the process of seeking further compensation.
